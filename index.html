A.M. Paradox
Christopher Brent Wood, better known by his stage names Brent Faiyaz, is an American singer and record producer. He released his debut project, an extended play entitled A.M. Paradox, in 2016.
Brent Faiyaz, So Far Gone



Faiyaz began uploading his music onto SoundCloud in 2014 and moved from his hometown Columbia, Maryland to Charlotte, North Carolina before ultimately settling in Los Angeles, California to further his music career. On January 10. 2015, he released his debut single "Allure".[4] On June 1, 2016, Faiyaz released "Invite Me", the lead single from this upcoming debut EP. On September 19, 2016, his 21st birthday, the EP entitled A.M. Paradox was released and received with positive reviews from music critics. On January 26, 2017, Sonder released their debut EP intro.
